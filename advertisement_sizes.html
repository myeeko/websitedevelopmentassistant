Choosing Between Responsive and Fixed-Size Ads

Responsive ads are the best choice for websites that need flexibility across different screen sizes, ensuring a smooth user experience. They adapt automatically, reducing the need for manual adjustments. On the other hand, fixed-size ads give you more control over placement and design, making them ideal for specific layouts.

For maximum revenue and user engagement, a combination of responsive and fixed-size ads can be beneficial. Test different placements to find what works best for your audience and website layout.
